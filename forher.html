The truth

You think I love you
a little — but the truth is,
I love you beyond measure.

There are feelings I have never been able to find words for. Feelings that sit quietly in my chest every time I see your name on my phone, every time I hear you laugh, every time you look at me and I forget how to breathe.

You have made me a better version of myself just by existing in my life. That is not something ordinary. That is something I choose to be grateful for, every single day.

♥

Why I love you

The list goes on forever,
but here are a few.

01
The way you care
You feel everything deeply — for people, for animals, for the world. That big heart of yours is the most beautiful thing I have ever seen.
02
Your smile
When you smile — truly smile — the whole room quietly shifts. It is warm. It is real. It is you, entirely.
03
You make home feel safe
Wherever you are, that is where I want to be. You are not just my love, you are the place I rest my mind.
04
Your strength
You carry so much, sometimes silently. But I see it. I see you. And I am in awe of you every single time.
05
The small moments
The random texts, the inside jokes, the comfortable silence — every small moment with you feels like a gift I did not deserve.
06
Simply being you
No performance. No pretend. Just you — beautifully, wonderfully, completely you. That is enough. That is more than enough.
